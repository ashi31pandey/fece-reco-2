About Us

Meet Our President

Dr. Kamal Ghanshala

Dr. Kamal Ghanshala is the visionary President of Graphic Era Group of Institutions. Under his leadership, Graphic Era has achieved remarkable milestones in the field of education, research, and innovation. His commitment to excellence has transformed Graphic Era into a prestigious university known for its academic rigor and vibrant campus life.

Dr. Ghanshala holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science and has numerous accolades to his name. His dedication to providing quality education has been instrumental in shaping the future of countless students.

Our History

Founded in 1993, Graphic Era University has grown from a small institution to a renowned university offering a wide range of programs. Our mission is to foster a culture of learning and innovation, preparing students to excel in their respective fields.
